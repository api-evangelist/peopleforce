PeopleForce explains how its HR efficiency estimator calculates time savings, cost reductions, and productivity gains using data from over 1,600 organizations. The tool factors in employee counts, HR staff size, and salary figures to produce hours saved, monthly efficiency value, and productivity boost percentage, while noting results vary by process maturity.
